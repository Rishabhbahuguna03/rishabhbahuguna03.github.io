01 · About

The story behind the analyst

I am a fresher targeting Product Analyst, Associate PM, Business Analyst, and Data Analyst roles — with a focus on understanding the data and reasoning behind product decisions, not just the decisions themselves.

My interest in product analytics started when I noticed that WhatsApp Pay was being pushed into an already-crowded UPI market. My first instinct wasn't "cool feature" — it was "what problem does this actually solve, and for whom?" That question led me to funnel thinking, retention metrics, user behaviour data, and the frameworks product teams use to justify — or sometimes obscure — their decisions.

My toolkit covers SQL, Python, Excel, and Tableau. But what I practise daily is the question behind the query: why did a metric move, which user segment is the story really about, and what one change would the data actually support?

I work on real-data projects, practise case studies on Unstop, and am building a portfolio that shows product thinking alongside technical skill. Available within 15 days — open to roles across India.
